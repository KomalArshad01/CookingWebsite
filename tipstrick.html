How to Speed Up Your Cooking Process

Prep ingredients ahead of time and use quick-cooking items like frozen veggies and canned beans. Invest in time-saving tools like a pressure cooker and sharp knives. One-pot meals also reduce cooking time and cleanup.

Ways to make vegetables longer in the fridge

Store vegetables in breathable bags or containers and keep leafy greens dry. Avoid washing before storage and place them in the crisper drawer. Wrap herbs in damp paper towels for extra freshness.

32 mins

Tools and gadgets that make cooking faster

To speed up cooking, use a pressure cooker, food processor, and sharp knives. A blender and slow cooker also save time on prep and cooking. These tools make meal prep quicker and easier.
